Programme and Event

Portfolio

A couple of programmes and events that I have joined in University Technology of Malaysia throughout my study.

BORNEO CONSUMER HEROES 2025

This programme is organized by Gerakan Pengguna Siswa UTM where we went to Papar, Sabah to visit school and village to educate the community consumerism. We spent 4 days and 3 nights in Sabah where we learned a lot about their culture especially their famous dance "poco-poco".

POGRAM ADVOKASI PENGGUNA CILIK: SPARKLE AND RECYCLE

We went to Tadika Kemas to engage with the students and community. The main objectives of this programme is to educate them about dental health and recycle. We spent the whole day in the kindergarten where we played games about recycling and demostation about dental health. It was a really fun day and I enjoyed it so much!
